Understanding

With our knowledge of power consumption comes a deep understanding of how best conserve power, use energy efficiently, saving real dollars and cents and helps to sustain the planet at the same time.

Our Approach

Use what you need, no more, no less

Fundamental to sustainability is the principle use what you need, widely accepted with regards to consumption, but why should an energy management system be any different?

We apply this principle to our service model. We do not wish to sell hardware, and by no means does one-size-fit-all. We offer an information-based system to deliver energy savings and evolve with your needs.

Tailored Service

A site will have different metering requirements at different stages of the efficiency spectrum. As a site improves, we scale our infrastructure, service and costs to directly meet your needs throughout this journey.

Minimal Capital

Enotels service requires no significant up-front capital with ongoing costs relevant to the continuing return on that service. Should you no longer require our services, there is no costly infrastructure left behind, ensuring maximum return on investment throughout the project.

Minimal Risk

All our services are offered on short-term contracts. Energy efficiency is a game of diminishing returns and the cost of energy management should reflect that.

How it works

Visibility

Having sub-meters provides for a granular visibility on consumption, enables building managers to identify sources of unnecessary energy usage and take action to reduce them.

Smart Design

Meters are located in targeted areas of high consumption or known behavioral opportunity. Metering strategy is fluid and adaptable based on consumption patterns and opportunities identified.

Smart Technology

Wireless RF monitoring technology allows for minimum disruption, shutdowns or communication cables. This permits for flexibility as usage and opportunities change.

Intuitive Reports

Existing operators have the experience and site knowledge, but they may not have the data on which to make efficiency decisions. By providing meaningful data, we aim to bridge that gap and provide an insight into how energy is used.

Hands On Approach

Using the information gathered and working collaboratively with site teams and contractors to implement solutions and reduce consumption.

How do we provide savings?

Behavioral Change

By metering specific areas, this information can be used to educate and inform building users of the true cost and impact of their behavior. Allowing them to take ownership of their usage.

Unnecessary Consumption

Often in large or complicated sites, the true source of consumption is not known. Visibility allows all usage of energy to be questioned, eliminating all unnecessary consumption.

Control Strategy Review

With any control, manual or automatic, for the HVAC or lighting there may be opportunity to fine-tune these strategies for efficiency gains. Independent verification of this control allows for a targeted approach.

Preventative Savings

When equipment or controllers fail, often energy usage increases. Having a dedicated monitoring system will reduce the response time to, or even predict, these events. Making shock bills a thing of the past.
